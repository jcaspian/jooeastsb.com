WELCOME TO JOOEASTSB.COM
Providing quality canopies since 2006.




JooEast Enterprise was founded by Mr. Tan Tee Joo in year 2006 selling and renting canopies. The main store was located in Kuantan Pahang. A year later, Mr. Tan expanded his business in Kuala Lumpur and started the first branch in Ampang. As the year goes by, Mr. Tan continued to expand his business and currently he owns 4 different branches all located in Kuala Lumpur.
